Play
- Place 1 scheme face down
- Reveal simultaneously
- If total time is more than player count, appoint left timeline scheme to court
- Take listed actions
- Appoint most powerful
- Everyone else takes 1 gold for each unique dungeon scheme
- Draw up to 3
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes left timeline scheme
- Buyer may take any schemes from court
- Imprison least powerful in court
Trade
- Trade face up schemes and gold
- When no one wants to, discard from least to most powerful
End
- If timeline is empty at round end, everyone plays 1 final scheme
- Points equals power on schemes in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest power scheme in hand
