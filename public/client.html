Place 1 scheme face down
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to raise, highest untied bidder takes left timeline scheme face up in front of them
- If only 1 scheme left in timeline, take that as well
- Buyer takes any schemes from the court face up in front of them
- Imprison lowest rank schemes in court into dungeon
Trade
- Everyone can trade face up schemes and gold
- When no one wants to trade, buyer takes their face up schemes in hand
Buyer discards down to 3
Meanwhile, everyone else plays their face up schemes in rank order:
- If total time on all plots in play equals player count or more, appoint left timeline scheme to court
- Take listed actions from lowest to highest rank
- Appoint highest rank schemes in play to court
- Take 1 gold for each scheme in the dungeon
- Discard face up schemes in order
- Draw up to 3
Ending the game
- If timeline is empty at round end, play 1 scheme face down with no auction
- Points equals power on schemes in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest rank scheme in hand
