Place 3 plots face down in a row
Play left plot
- Take listed actions
- If total time is 3 per player or more, pay left plot in timeline to court
- Lowest ranked take 1 gold for each plot in the court.
- Then, lowest ranked pay their plot to the court.
- Discard remaining plots
Auction
- Reveal next plot
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to raise, highest untied bidder takes the highest ranked court plot and left timeline plot face up in front of them
- Buyer may take any plots from court face up in front of them
Trade
- Trade gold and revealed plots
- When nobody wants to trade, everyone takes revealed plots into their hand
- Buyer discards down to 5
- Everyone draws up to 5
Play last face down plot
Ending the game
- If timeline is empty at round end, place 2 plots face down
- Play left
- Play next
- Points equals power on plots in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest rank plot in hand
