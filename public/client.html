Play
- Place 2 schemes face down
- Reveal leftmost simultaneously
- If total time is more than player count, appoint left timeline scheme to court
- Take listed actions
- Appoint highest rank
- Draw until you have at least 3 schemes in hand
- Discard remaining card
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount of gold at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes left timeline scheme
- Buyer may take any schemes from court
- Imprison lowest rank from court to dungeon
Trade
- Trade face up schemes and gold
- When no one wants to, discard from lowest to highest rank
End
- If timeline is empty at round end, everyone plays 1 final scheme
- Points equals rank on schemes in hand
- 1 point for each gold and note 
- Tiebreaker is highest rank scheme in hand
