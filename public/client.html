Place 2 plots face down
Everyone reveals their first plot.
Play left face down plot
- First, if total time equals player count or more, appoint left plot in timeline to court
- Then, take listed actions
- Next, highest ranked appoint their plots to the court.
- Finally, everyone else takes 1 gold for each plot in the dungeon
Discard remaining face up plots
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to raise, highest untied bidder takes left timeline plot face up in front of them
- If only 1 plot left in timeline, take that as well
- Buyer takes any cards from the court face up in front of them
- Imprison lowest rank plots in court into dungeon
Reveal next face down plot
Trade
- Everyone can trade face up cards and gold
- When no one wants to trade, buyer takes their face up plots in hand
Buyer discards down to 3
Meanwhile, everyone else plays their face up plots in rank order:
- First, if total time on all plots in play equals player count or more, appoint left timeline plot to court
- Then, take listed actions from lowest to highest rank
- Next, highest ranked of all plots in play appoint their plots to the court
- Finally, everyone else takes 1 gold for each plot in the dungeon
Take remaining face up plots in hand
Ending the game
- If timeline is empty at round end, play 1 plot face down with no auction
- Points equals power on plots in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest rank plot in hand
