Place 3 plots face down
Everyone reveals their first plot.
Play left face down plot
- If total time equals player count or more, appoint left plot in timeline to court
- Take listed actions
- Everyone else takes 1 gold for each unique plot in the dungeon
- Then, highest ranked appoint their plots to the court.
- Discard remaining played plots
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to raise, highest untied bidder takes left timeline plot face up in front of them.
- If only 1 plot left in timeline, take that as well.
- Buyer takes any cards from the court in front of them
Trading
- Reveal next face down plot
- Everyone can trade face up cards and gold
- Everyone takes their face up plots into their hand
Imprison lowest rank plots in court into dungeon
Buyer takes remaining face down plot into their hand
Buyer discards down to 3, while everyone else plays their remaining plot.
Everyone except the buyer draws up to 5.
Ending the game
- If timeline is empty at round end, play 1 plot face down with no auction
- Points equals power on plots in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest rank plot in hand
