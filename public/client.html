Play
- Place 1 scheme face down
- Reveal simultaneously
- If total time equals player count or more, appoint left timeline scheme
- Take listed actions
- Appoint highest rank schemes
- Everyone else takes 1 gold for each dungeon scheme
- Draw up to 3
Auction
- Raise bid by any amount at any time
- Withdraw if tied
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes left timeline scheme
- Buyer may take any schemes from court
- Imprison lowest rank schemes in court
Trade
- Trade face up schemes and gold
- When no one wants to trade, discard least powerful
- Buyer discards all from lowest to highest
- Everyone else discards lowest and takes remaining in hand
End
- If timeline is empty at round end, game immediately ends
- Points equals power on schemes in hand
- 1 point for each gold
- Tiebreaker is highest rank scheme in hand
